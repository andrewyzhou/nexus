Our Upwork Marketplace platform contains a large repository of closed-loop data for the entire lifecycle of work, starting from when clients post projects to when talent and clients match, how they communicate via on-platform messaging and collaboration tools, how and when payment is transferred, and feedback on their engagements. Scalability Our cloud-based Upwork Marketplace platform has been designed to be elastic, scaling automatically with increased usage, supporting sudden traffic spikes by dynamically bringing additional capacity online as required, then scaling back to ensure consistent and predictable cost management.
