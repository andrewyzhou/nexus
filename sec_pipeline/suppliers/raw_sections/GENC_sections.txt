Seasonality The Company is concentrated in the manufacturing of asphalt pavers, asphalt plants and related components, which is typically subject to a seasonal slow-down during the third and fourth quarters of the calendar year. Competition The markets for the Company’s products are highly competitive. The industry remains fairly concentrated, with a small number of companies competing in the markets of the majority of the Company’s product lines. The principal competitive factors include quality, price, delivery, availability, financing, and technological capabilities. The Company believes it manufactures the highest quality equipment in the industry.
