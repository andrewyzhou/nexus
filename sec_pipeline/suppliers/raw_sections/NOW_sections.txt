Workflow Data Fabric Workflow Data Fabric (“WDF”) provides organizations the ability to use and access a variety of data types—structured or unstructured, streaming or static—both within and outside our Platform. Built on the foundation laid by our legacy Automation Engine product, WDF supports data integrations with a large network of certified partners and a broad set of data integration capabilities, including connecting to real-time external data without copying or moving the data from its source. Additionally, WDF provides users of our Platform with an interface, known as a semantic layer, that helps users interpret and use data to complete tasks without requiring technical knowledge of underlying database structures.
