Leverage ratios are widely used by investors, analysts, and rating agencies in the valuation, comparison, rating, and investment recommendations of companies. Although leverage ratios are commonly-used financial measures, definitions differ. The Company calculates leverage as adjusted debt divided by trailing twelve month adjusted EBITDAR.
