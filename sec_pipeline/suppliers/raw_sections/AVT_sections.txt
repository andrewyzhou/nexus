Avnet Supply Chain Solutions EC’s supply chain solutions provide procurement support and warehousing and logistics services to OEMs, EMS providers, and electronic component manufacturers, enabling them to optimize supply chains on a local, regional, or global basis. EC’s internal competencies in supply chain, global warehousing and logistics, information technology, and asset management, combined with its global footprint and extensive supplier relationships, allows EC to develop supply chain solutions that provide for a deeper level of engagement with its customers.
