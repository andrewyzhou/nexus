Heavy Materials Our Heavy Materials sector provides cement and concrete and aggregates for use in public infrastructure, private nonresidential, and residential construction. This sector comprises the Cement and Concrete and Aggregates segments. In calendar 2024, cement consumption in the United States, as estimated by the Portland Cement Association (PCA), was approximately 111.4 million short tons, which was approximately 6% lower than calendar 2023 consumption levels. While cement consumption was down in the U.S., the U.S. still consumes more cement than we can domestically produce. Imported cement consumption represented 25% of total sales in both calendar 2024 and 2023. CEMENT Cement is the basic binding agent for concrete, a primary construction material. The principal sources of demand for cement are public infrastructure, private nonresidential construction, and residential construction, with public infrastructure accounting for nearly 50% of cement demand. The U.S. cement industry comprises numerous regional markets rather than a single national market. Cement consumption is affected by the time of year and prevalent weather conditions.
