We currently compete with established and early stage life sciences and in vitro diagnostics companies developing or commercializing flow cytometry instruments and consumables, as well as other companies that design, manufacture and market instruments, accessories, consumables, reagent kits and software for, among other applications, cell analysis, immunophenotyping, cell sorting and/or provide services related to the same.
