The Company’s financial instruments consist of cash, accounts receivable, deposits, and accounts payable and accrued liabilities and derivative liability. The fair value of the Company’s financial instruments other than the derivative liability approximate their carrying value due to the short-term nature of their maturity. The Company’s financial instruments initially measured at fair value and then held at amortized cost include cash, accounts receivable, deposits, and accounts payable and accrued liabilities. The majority of the Company’s cash is held with two Canadian Financial Institutions and is uninsured as at November 30, 2025.
