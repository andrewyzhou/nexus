We accrue for the estimated future costs of reimbursing our workers' compensation and health insurance carriers under our insurance policies. We use internal actuaries to develop health claims estimates, and we use external actuaries and our own experience to develop workers’ compensation estimates, but the volume and severity of claims activity is difficult to accurately predict.
