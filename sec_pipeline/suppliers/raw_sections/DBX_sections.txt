We employ strong protections for all of the data on our platform. • Encryption . Dropbox file data at rest is encrypted using 256-bit Advanced Encryption Standard, or AES. To protect data in transit between Dropbox apps such as desktop, mobile, API, or web and our servers, Dropbox uses Secure Sockets Layer, or SSL, and Transport Layer Security, or TLS, for data transfer, creating a secure tunnel protected by 128-bit or higher AES encryption. • File recovery . Every deletion event in Dropbox is recorded, including when groups of files are deleted. Users can easily recover files through our web interface.
