These risks include, among other things: operational risks, intellectual property and proprietary business information theft, fraud, extortion, harm to employees or customers, violation of privacy or security laws and other litigation and legal risk, physical damage to utility and transmission infrastructure, and reputational harm. We have implemented cybersecurity processes, technologies, and controls to aid in our efforts to assess, identify, and manage these risks.
